We collect various types of information for different purposes to provide and improve our services.

When you use our services or interact with us, we may ask you to provide certain personal data that can be used to identify or contact you ("personal data"). Personal data may include, but is not limited to:

We may also collect information about how our services are accessed and used ("usage data"). 1.1.3 Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information and improve your experience with our services. Cookies are small files that contain an anonymous unique identifier and are transferred by a web server to your computer's hard drive. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some parts of our services.
